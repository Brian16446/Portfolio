About this project:

This project was for my final coursework for my third year of university and is the first android app i've created. The app features a list of ingredients the user can choose from, as well as a search bar at the top that filters the ingredients listed. The user can tap ingredients to add them to a list and tap the submit button to load a list of drinks the user can make based on the ingredients they chose. They can then tap on a cocktail from the list to view information about the cocktail such as how to make it.
